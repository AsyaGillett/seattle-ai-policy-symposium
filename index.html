Welcome

The Seattle AI Policy Symposium invites students and educators from independent schools across the Seattle area to Lakeside for a few hours of honest conversation about what is and is not working in our current AI policies.

At the end of the day, many of us are graded on similar transcripts, apply to many of the same colleges, and are navigating the same confusing questions about AI. This symposium is a chance to compare approaches, spark curiosity, and draft something useful together.
