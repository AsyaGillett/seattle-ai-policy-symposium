FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission?

To bring Seattle-area independent schools together to discuss current AI policies and draft shared recommendations that make AI expectations more fair, clear, and useful.

Is the goal one identical policy for every school?

The ideal outcome is a shared regional framework: common principles and expectations that schools can reference while still adapting to their own communities.

Who is this conference for?

Students and educators who care about AI, grading, academic integrity, equity, and the future of learning.

What will participants do?

Participants will compare policies, discuss satisfaction and dissatisfaction with current rules, join roundtables, and help draft a shared document of ideas and recommendations.

What should each school bring?

Each school should bring its current AI policy, any relevant rubrics, and honest reflections about what is working and what is not.

What happens after the symposium?

The organizing team will compile takeaways into a student- and educator-informed document that participating schools can use when revising their policies.
